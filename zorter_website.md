## Detailed Solutions <a id="solutions"></a>
<!-- Layout: Alternating sections or Tabs for each solution -->

### 1. Zorter Infirmary (Hospital Management)
*State-of-the-art hospital automation software.*

**Key Features:**
*   **Comprehensive Care**: OPD, IPD, Pathology, Radiology, Blood Bank.
*   **Administration**: Front Office, Billing & Reception, Ambulance Services.
*   **Records**: Birth & Death Records, Referral Management, TPA Management.
*   **Communication**: Messaging System, Live Consultation, Patient Chat.
*   **User Roles**: 8 inbuilt roles including Super Admin, Doctor, Accountant, Pathologist, Radiologist, Receptionist, and Patient.

### 2. Zorter School (School Management)
*Modern, comprehensive school automation.*

**Key Features:**
*   **Academics**: Attendance, Timetable, Examination & Online Tests.
*   **Administration**: Student Admission, Fee Management (with penalties/discounts), Library, Hostel, Transport.
*   **Portals**: Dedicated Parent & Student Panels for real-time access to grades and fees.
*   **Communication**: Messaging between students, parents, and teachers.

### 3. Zorter ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)
*Simplify accounting, inventory, and operations.*

**Key Features:**
*   **Financials**: Accounting, GST/VAT, Expense Management, Trial Balance, P&L.
*   **Operations**: Inventory Management, Manufacturing, Supply Chain, Procurement.
*   **Multi-Support**: Multiple Businesses & Locations, Multi-Currency.
*   **HR**: Human Resources Management.

### 4. Zorter CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
*Analyze interactions and improve relationships.*

**Key Features:**
*   **Lead Management**: Track sources, life stages, and convert leads to customers.
*   **Follow-ups**: Track follow-ups with leads/customers (Pending/Partial/Overdue).
*   **Campaigns**: Email and SMS marketing.
*   **Client Portal**: Login access for customers/suppliers to view orders and ledgers.

### 5. Zorter E-Commerce
*Expand your business globally.*

**Key Features:**
*   **Global Reach**: Access diverse markets locally and internationally.
*   **Efficiency**: Automated inventory oversight, streamlined purchasing.
*   **Growth**: Scalable platform, data-driven consumer insights.
*   **Security**: Robust measures to protect sensitive data.
